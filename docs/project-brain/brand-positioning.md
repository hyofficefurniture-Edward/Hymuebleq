# Brand Positioning

- Last updated: 2026-06-29
- Brand name in the site frontend: Hymueble
- Language: neutral Latin American Spanish

## Positioning

Hymueble is a Spanish-language B2B furniture project solutions brand for buyers who need coordinated furniture supply from China for commercial, hospitality, office, healthcare, education, and residential development projects.

The site should not behave like a retail furniture catalog. It should communicate project capacity, manufacturing coordination, quality control, export preparation, and reduced procurement risk.

## Target Customers

- Hotel owners and hotel procurement teams
- Office building owners and corporate procurement teams
- Architects, interior designers, and contractors
- Real estate developers and residential project teams
- Furniture distributors, importers, and project buyers
- Healthcare, education, restaurant, and commercial-space operators

## Core Trust Points

- Direct factory coordination in China
- Project-based furniture supply, not isolated SKU selling
- Customization by drawings, materials, dimensions, and finishes
- Quality control before packing and export
- Export packaging and logistics support
- Project references in hospitality, education, and other B2B spaces
- Virtual Showroom for remote product and space review

## Business Boundary

Hymueble should promise project consultation, quotation, production coordination, quality-control process, and export support. It should not claim a real backend, a real WhatsApp number, or verified certificate details until those are provided and wired into the site.

## Differentiation

The brand must feel more like an international project furniture partner than a commodity factory listing. The difference is shown through:

- Spatial project imagery over product-only grids
- Clear sector routes
- Factory and process evidence
- Strong showroom entry
- Professional Spanish copy that speaks to buyer risk, lead times, documentation, and project clarity
