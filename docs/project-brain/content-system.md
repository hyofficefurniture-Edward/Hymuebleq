# Content System

- Last updated: 2026-06-29
- Language: Spanish for Latin American B2B buyers

## Copy Voice

The Spanish copy must be:

- Direct
- Professional
- Project-oriented
- Clear about manufacturing and export
- Useful for procurement and design teams
- Free of machine-translation tone
- Free of vague prestige claims

## Core Messages

- `Muebles para proyectos B2B`
- `Soluciones de mobiliario para hoteles, oficinas y proyectos inmobiliarios`
- `Coordinación de producción, control de calidad y soporte desde China`
- `Menos riesgo, más claridad en cada etapa del proyecto`
- `Explora soluciones en nuestro showroom virtual`
- `Cotizaciones para proyectos comerciales`
- `Acompañamiento en selección, producción y control de calidad`

## CTA Library

- `Cotizar` — short Header CTA only, used where horizontal space is limited.
- `Solicitar cotización`
- `Enviar requerimientos del proyecto`
- `Explorar showroom virtual`
- `Ver showroom virtual`
- `Ver soluciones por proyecto`
- `Conocer capacidades de fábrica`
- `Solicitar catálogo`
- `Consultar un proyecto similar`

## Page Copy Rules

- Lead with project value, not company bragging.
- Avoid standalone product-list language unless tied to space, sector, or procurement decision.
- Use proof before persuasion: process, factory, inspection, project references, showroom.
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
- Keep Header CTAs short and balanced; long WhatsApp text buttons belong in page sections or footer, not in the main navigation.
- Use the fixed WhatsApp icon as the persistent global WhatsApp entry, while page copy can still mention WhatsApp in context.
- Avoid overpromising exact response time unless operationally confirmed; current preview can keep the planned 24h language as a placeholder-style business promise to be verified before launch.

## Banned Low-Quality Expressions

- Generic phrases like `alta calidad y buen servicio` without context.
- Empty slogans like `su mejor opción`.
- Retail language that makes Hymueble sound like a consumer furniture shop.
- Overloaded keyword stuffing.
- Visible internal terms such as `primera versión`, `placeholder`, `老板演示`, or `资质`.
