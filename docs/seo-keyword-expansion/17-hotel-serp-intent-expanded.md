## SERP conclusion

- Hotel industry terms have more opportunity than the previous 0-50 map suggested, but the opportunity is split: `muebles para hoteles` and product/space terms carry volume, while supplier/factory/conversion terms carry intent.
- High-volume product terms must be contained inside qualified hotel-space pages. Unqualified `sillas para restaurante`, `mesas para restaurante`, `muebles para exteriores` and `muebles para recepción` are not safe page primaries.
- Low-volume terms such as `proveedor de muebles para hoteles`, `mobiliario hotelero a medida`, `cotización de muebles para hoteles` and China/import terms are commercially valuable as CTA, proof and FAQ language.
